Irish Food

Irish food is both filling and comforting while being a direct reflection of the country's agriculture. Potatoes are a staple that are featured in dishes and on the side with stews as well. Traditional Irish stews have meat, root vegetables, and a rich broth. Seafood is also very popular because of Ireland's coast, such as salmon and mussels. Soda bread is a quick loaf of bread that features raisins and is made with baking.

One featured meal is Irish breakfast. Traditional Irish breakfast is a very heavy meal, with items like sausages, black and white pudding, Irish bacon (thicker, ham-like bacon), eggs, and grilled tomatoes. Many visitors are misdirected by this breakfast as it is much heavier and richer than other countries' cuisine.
